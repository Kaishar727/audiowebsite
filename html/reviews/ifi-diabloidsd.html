Amplifiers

Diablo IDSD

The iFi Audio Diablo iDSD is a high-performance portable DAC and headphone amplifier designed to elevate your audio experience on the go. With its sleek and compact design, this device boasts top-tier components and advanced features, making it a powerhouse for audiophiles seeking premium sound quality from their headphones.

Color
Sound: The Diablo iDSD delivers an exceptional audio experience, characterized by a transparent, detailed, and dynamic sound signature. With its dual-mono construction and Burr-Brown DAC chips, it provides an immersive listening experience, offering a wide soundstage and precise instrument separation.
Design: The compact and stylish design of the Diablo iDSD combines robust build quality with portability. The sleek aluminum chassis houses a variety of inputs and controls, including a powerful 4.4mm balanced output. The device's design reflects both functionality and aesthetics, catering to audio enthusiasts with discerning tastes.
Price: Positioned in the premium segment, the Diablo iDSD reflects its quality through its price. While it may be on the higher end of the market, the device's audio performance justifies the investment for those seeking top-tier sound quality in a portable package.
Performance: The Diablo iDSD excels in performance, supporting high-resolution audio formats and featuring a powerful headphone amplifier. Its versatility extends to compatibility with various headphones, making it an ideal choice for audiophiles who demand uncompromising performance from their portable audio setup.

Final Rating

92

The iFi Audio Diablo iDSD earns its acclaim as a top-tier portable DAC and headphone amplifier. With an overall score of 9.25/10, it impresses with its impeccable sound quality, stylish design, and versatile features. While the premium price may be a consideration, the Diablo iDSD stands as a premium option for those who prioritize audiophile-grade audio performance in a portable form factor.
